Client: Northwind Trail Outfitters
Project: GA4 + Search Console Visibility Program
Effective Date: April 1, 2026
Service Provider: Everyday Workflows

1. Scope of Services

Everyday Workflows will configure and deliver the following work for Northwind Trail Outfitters:

  • Google Analytics 4 property review and event mapping
  • Google Search Console verification and sitemap review
  • Dashboard setup for weekly executive reporting
  • Monthly performance summary with recommendations

2. Deliverables

The Service Provider will deliver the following items during the initial engagement period:

  1. Analytics implementation checklist
  2. KPI dashboard with traffic, conversion, and CTR trends
  3. Search performance snapshot for top landing pages
  4. One strategy call per month

3. Timeline

Implementation begins within five business days of signature. Initial setup is expected to conclude within fourteen business days, subject to timely access to required accounts.

4. Fees

The Client agrees to the following fees:

Item Amount
Initial setup $2,500
Monthly reporting retainer $850

Invoices are due within seven calendar days of issue.

5. Client Responsibilities

The Client will provide:

  • Access to the website CMS or tag manager
  • Access to Google Analytics and Google Search Console
  • A point of contact for approvals within two business days

6. Confidentiality

Both parties will keep non-public business, customer, and technical information confidential and will use that information only for the purpose of fulfilling this agreement.

7. Term and Termination

Either party may terminate the monthly reporting retainer with fourteen days written notice. Fees for services already performed remain due.

8. Signatures

By signing, both parties agree to the terms of this Mock Service Agreement.
